Every body is a yoga body

Claudia’s joy and passion for yoga are palpable and infectious. She has had the honor and privilege to have studied with many amazing teachers in various parts of the world. Her style brings together elements of strength, flexibility and balance, encouraging students to break through their perceived limitations both on and off the mat. 

With a background in Business Management, she struggled before with constant overthinking and nervousness, getting out of her head and into her body, Claudia soon found that Yoga practice dropped her into her body in ways she could begin to see the blossoming of deeper levels of self-understanding.

When she had a chance to start a second career, she dedicated herself to learning and teaching the practice of Yoga.  What began as a purely physical practice led Claudia to discover the philosophy behind this amazing practice and mental benefits of a well-rounded yoga practice.

It is through pranayama, asanas and meditation that she taps into the sense of inner calm and self-acceptance that yoga brings, and she encourages her students to do the same. Her classes encourage exploration, safety, and acceptance, honoring the body where it is in the present moment.

She believes that everyone deserves to live a healthy, peaceful and happy life and that regular Yoga practice is instrumental to make that happen.
