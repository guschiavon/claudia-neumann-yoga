Why take your yoga beyond the studio?
It is common for new students to join a group yoga class and feel overwhelmed or out of place. In studio classes, instructors are simply not able to address the unique needs of every student.
Perfect choice for beginners to yoga!
Beginners to yoga require clear explanations and patient instruction to effectively learn the basics. Students with injuries must also learn safe alignment techniques and proper modifications. I can provide hands-on assists and individualized adjustments to ensure proper and safe alignment.
But also for advanced yogis and yoginis!
There’s mainly two reasons a private class could serve your needs: Time and Personal Development. Your lifestyle might have changed (e.g. a new job or a baby) and you miss your yoga classes because you don’t know how to fit them into your schedule? Your private lessons will be scheduled at a time and location that is most convenient for you. No more stressing about getting to class on time! The other reason might be that you’re coming to your edge at studio classes and you’d like to master some poses like headstand, deep diver into meditation or work on personal development then a private yoga class is perfect for you!
